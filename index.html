About the Laboratory

The Cancer Biology and Imaging Laboratory investigates molecular mechanisms underlying cancer development using cytogenetics, genomics, and computational biology. Our research integrates experimental and computational approaches to discover biomarkers and therapeutic targets.

Research Focus

Cancer Genomics

Gene expression and multi-omics analysis.

Cytogenetics

Chromosomal instability and karyotype analysis.

Computational Drug Discovery

Molecular docking and structure-based design.

AI in Cancer Biology

Machine learning models for biomarker discovery.
